Demeter is a new brand of trendy and casual clothing that allows its buyers to have a wide range of different clothes produced and designed in France. It is one of the new pioneers of fashion and allows its buyers to be part of a new ethical fashion.The logic of the products is simple, we reuse the surgical masks and transform them into clothes instead of burning them at the waste center. It is an approach that is both ecological and economical, because the near end of the covid makes the price of the masks drop significantly.
